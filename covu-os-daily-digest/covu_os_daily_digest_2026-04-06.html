Executive Summary

1,840 services were created and 10,020 tasks generated across the platform today. 2,192 tasks were completed successfully, while 277 block events occurred across 242 unique tasks, representing a 2.4% block rate. The dominant block pattern from agent notes was wrong agent/team assignment (64%), including tasks routed to CL instead of PL teams, COIs delivered to wrong agents, and direct-billed policy tasks requiring no action. On the support front, agents reported page auto-refresh disruptions (erasing email drafts mid-composition), tasks disappearing post-triage during live calls, and unexpected availability status flips. Engineering shipped 7 merged PRs to covu-os (observability dashboards, Salesforce sync, playbook import hardening), moved 10 items in release 1.0.40 to Ready for QA, and resolved 2 highest-priority bugs. 50 Jira issues were updated today across the OS project.
